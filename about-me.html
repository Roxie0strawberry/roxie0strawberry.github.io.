TL;DR of My Career Journey ↓

All for the plot - Know more here

  • 2009-2011 Began my cultural immersion journey by living and studying in Rockville, Maryland, USA. This experience fostered fluency in English and broadened my horizons.
  • 2011-2015 Developed strong adaptability skills due to attending various schools in Brazil as a child of a navy officer. This fostered a resourceful and flexible approach to challenges.
  • 2015-2019 Earned a Bachelors in Film from Universidade Beira Interior in Portugal. During this time, I explored my passion for storytelling, editing, and sound design, skills highly valuable in creative communication.
  • 2010-2020 (Blogger, Self-employed) Built a thriving online community through blogging and content creation. This experience honed my social media management skills and deepened my understanding of online communities, valuable assets in today's digital marketing landscape.
  • 2014-2020 (Content Creator, Freelance) I've consistently demonstrated my passion for content creation and social media management through a long-standing freelance career. This experience has fostered a strong community of followers and a commitment to continuous learning within the ever-evolving digital landscape.
  • 2019-2021 Further honed my communication skills by earning a Master's degree in Strategic Communication, Marketing, and PR from Universidade Beira Interior. Additionally, I gained practical experience launching a game studio with my husband and friends, contributing in marketing and content creation.
  • 2020-Present (Chief Marketing Officer, Untitled Game Studios) Currently lead all marketing, publicity, sales, and branding efforts for my indie game studio. This role allows me to leverage my diverse skill set to manage online presence, social media, and launch-related activities for successful project execution.
  • 2021-2022 (Resilience and Professional Growth) This period presented significant personal challenges due to family losses. Demonstrating resilience, I navigated these difficulties while actively pursuing professional growth. I strategically adjusted my workload, prioritizing key projects like Droian and Untitled Game Studios while maintaining select social media clients. This focus allowed me to dedicate myself to learning web development. Within a few months, I mastered the fundamentals, independently building and launching two websites. This achievement solidified my career shift towards web development, and I'm excited to leverage this new skillset in future endeavors.
  • 2022-2023 (Creative Digital Marketing Manager & Community Expert, Freelance) Expanded my marketing skillset by managing B2B and B2C campaigns, web design, email marketing, social media, community engagement, website redesigns, development of E-commerce websites and more.
  • 2023 (Digital Architect, Half Three - Freelance) Leveraged my communication and marketing expertise to manage social media, content creation, branding, and website development for clients.
  • 2023-Present (Droian - Metroidvania Game, Untitled Game Studios) Successfully led various aspects of the Droian game project, showcasing my multifaceted skillset. I spearheaded branding, visual arts, content creation, online presence management, and project management to bring this exciting game experience to life.
  • 2024 (FreeCodeCamp) Committed to continuous learning by recently completing courses in Introduction to Web Design and UX/UI for Beginners. This additional skillset further enhances my ability to deliver engaging and user-friendly online experiences.
